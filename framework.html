CRITICAL SHIFT: Your leads are not "Inbound Hand-Raisers." They are "Outbound Interruptions."

When a senior snaps "You called me!" they are really saying: "I feel out of control and I don't know who you are."

False Expectation Trap
Never say "I understand you'd like information." That is a lie in their mind. Use the "Assignment Frame" instead.
Status Imbalance
Don't start from zero. You are not starting a conversation; you are continuing one as the Specialist.
The "Opt-Out" Fix
"How are you?" is a signal to hang up. Use the "Confusion Clearer" to force engagement.
